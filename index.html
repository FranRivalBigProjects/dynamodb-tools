❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hot partition in DynamoDB?

A partition receiving disproportionate traffic, causing throttling and latency issues.

How can I reduce DynamoDB costs?

Optimize capacity usage, indexing, and access patterns.

What is throughput in DynamoDB?

The number of read and write operations a table can handle.

What are GSIs and LSIs?

Secondary indexes used to optimize query patterns.
